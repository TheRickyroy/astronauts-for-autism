Safeguarding Children

Ensuring safety and well-being

For Astronauts

Need Help?

Whether big or small, the AFA team is always here to help.

Please speak to any member of team today.

Overwhelmed?

That's OK, you can take a break at any time.

Let's try one of the techniques we have learned together.

  • Box Breathing
  • Butterfly Hug
  • Shake Those Icky Feelings

Bad Behaviour?

Have you, or someone you know been made to feel uncomfortable, or scared?

Speak to a member of the team.

Stay Safe Online

  • Think before you post
  • Think about who you are talking to
  • Never share your password or personal details
  • Cover your webcam when you aren't using it

Don't Go Hungry

Sometimes it can be difficult to eat, but we have to remember how important it is.

If you are going hungry, or have been struggling to eat, come talk to us.

Need Advice?

Worried about something, or just need to talk we're always here.

Our Safeguarding Policies

We are committed to providing a safe, supportive and encouraging environment for children and families that protects from harm, abuse and neglect.

Everyone deserves to feel safe, which is why safeguarding is a fundamental part of our mission here at Astronauts for Autism.

For more information please speak to a member of our team, or alternatively, you can download a PDF of our Safeguarding Policy below.
